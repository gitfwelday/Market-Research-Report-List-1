<p><p>The self-healing polymer market is categorized into three main types: vascular, intrinsic, and capsule-based systems. Vascular systems incorporate channels that carry healing agents, allowing for targeted repair when damage occurs. Intrinsic systems possess built-in properties enabling them to automatically mend minor injuries without external aid. Capsule-based systems contain microcapsules filled with healing agents that release upon rupture, initiating the healing process. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods.</p></p>
