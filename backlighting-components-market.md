<p><p>Backlighting components are essential in various display technologies, and they primarily come in three market types: Edge Lighting, Bottom Lighting, and Hollow Type. Edge Lighting utilizes LEDs positioned along the edges of a panel to create uniform brightness, ideal for slim designs. Bottom Lighting involves LEDs placed at the bottom, providing an even light distribution across the display surface. Hollow Type integrates light sources within a cavity, maximizing space efficiency while ensuring effective illumination, suitable for innovative display applications.</p></p>
