Wood pulp-based MCC is derived from natural plant fibers, offering cost-effectiveness and stability, while refined cotton-based MCC is sourced from high-quality cotton fibers, known for its purity and optimal performance in dry formulations. Both types are essential in ensuring product efficacy and consistency in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
