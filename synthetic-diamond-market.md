<p><p>The synthetic diamond market comprises various diamond grain types, each serving distinct industrial applications. RVD (Regularly Varied Diamond) grains are primarily utilized for cutting tools due to their robustness. MBD (Micro-Small Diamond) grains are favored for precision grinding in electronics. SCD (Single Crystal Diamond) grains provide superior hardness for specialized applications. SMD (Single-Molecule Diamond) grains cater to niche uses in advanced manufacturing. DMD (Diamond Micro Powder) grains are used for polishing and surface finishing in various industries, enhancing product durability.</p></p>
