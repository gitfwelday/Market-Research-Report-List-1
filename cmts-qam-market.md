<p><ul><li>Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS)</li><li>Modular CMTS (M-CMTS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMTS (Cable Modem Termination System) market consists of Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS) solutions, both essential for delivering broadband services over cable networks. I-CMTS combines all functions into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of deployment. In contrast, M-CMTS separates functions into modules, allowing for scalability and flexibility in network expansion. Both types address varying operator needs, with I-CMTS suitable for smaller networks and M-CMTS for larger, more complex infrastructures.</p></p>
