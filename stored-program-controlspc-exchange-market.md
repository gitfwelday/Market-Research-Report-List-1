<p><p>Stored Program Control (SPC) exchanges enable automated switching in telecommunications systems. In a Centralized SPC market, all control functions are managed from a single location, allowing for efficient resource allocation and simplified management but can lead to single points of failure. Conversely, a Distributed SPC market decentralizes control across multiple nodes, enhancing reliability and scalability while reducing vulnerability. This structure supports localized decision-making, improving responsiveness and adaptability to varying demand within telecommunications networks.</p></p>
