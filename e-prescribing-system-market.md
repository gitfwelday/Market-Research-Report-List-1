<p><p>E-Prescribing systems can be categorized into two main market types: stand-alone systems and integrated systems. Stand-alone systems operate independently, allowing healthcare providers to send prescriptions electronically without needing to connect to other healthcare software. In contrast, integrated systems are part of a broader electronic health record (EHR) platform, facilitating seamless data exchange and enhanced patient management. Both options streamline the prescribing process, improve medication accuracy, and enhance patient safety, but their suitability varies based on healthcare provider needs and infrastructure.</p></p>
