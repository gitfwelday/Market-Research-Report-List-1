<p><strong>The ESD TVS Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Junction Capacitive Type</li><li>Low Junction Capacitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ESD TVS diode market is categorized into high junction capacitive and low junction capacitive types, each serving distinct applications in protecting sensitive electronic components. High junction capacitive types offer greater capacitance, suitable for high-speed data lines where signal integrity is crucial, while low junction capacitive types have reduced capacitance, making them ideal for RF applications and high-frequency systems. The choice between these types depends on the balance between effective ESD protection and minimal impact on circuit performance.</p></p>
