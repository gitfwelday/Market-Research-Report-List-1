<p><p>The desktop sterilizers market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Steam Sterilizers** use saturated steam under pressure to eliminate microorganisms, making them effective for heat-resistant materials.</p><p>2. **Plasma Sterilizers** utilize hydrogen peroxide gas energized into plasma, suitable for heat-sensitive instruments.</p><p>3. **Hot Air Sterilizers** apply dry heat for sterile processing, primarily used for glassware and metal instruments.</p><p>4. **Others** encompass alternative methods like ultraviolet (UV) light and chemical sterilization, catering to specific requirements in laboratories and medical environments.</p></p>
