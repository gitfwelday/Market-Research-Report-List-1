<p><p>The Mobile Speech Recognition Software market comprises three main types: Isolated Word Recognition, Keyword Spotting, and Continuous Speech Recognition. Isolated Word Recognition focuses on recognizing single words spoken clearly, often used in command-based systems. Keyword Spotting identifies specific words or phrases within continuous speech, enabling targeted responses to user commands. Continuous Speech Recognition processes full sentences and conversational speech, allowing for more natural interactions and broader application in virtual assistants and dictation software, enhancing user experience across mobile devices.</p></p>
