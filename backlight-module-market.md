<p><ul><li>CCFL Backlight Module</li><li>LED Backlight Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backlight module market primarily includes two types: CCFL (Cold Cathode Fluorescent Lamp) and LED (Light Emitting Diode) backlight modules. CCFL backlight modules utilize fluorescent lamps to produce light, offering even illumination but consuming more power and being bulkier. In contrast, LED backlight modules leverage semiconductor technology for illumination, providing improved energy efficiency, thinner designs, and better brightness control. The shift towards LED technology is driven by its advantages in performance, lifespan, and environmental impact.</p></p>
