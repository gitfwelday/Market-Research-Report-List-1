<p><strong>The Single Phase Welder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC</li><li>DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase welder market is primarily categorized into two types: AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current) welders. AC welders are ideal for welding materials like aluminum and magnesium, providing a smooth, fluctuating arc, making them suitable for thinner metals. Conversely, DC welders offer a steady arc with better penetration and control, making them preferable for thicker metals and applications requiring precision. Both types cater to different industrial and DIY needs, influencing their market demand and pricing dynamics.</p></p>
