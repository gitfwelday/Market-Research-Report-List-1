<p><strong>The Centrifugal Heart Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Devices</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal heart pumps market is categorized into two main types: implantable heart pump devices and extracorporeal heart pump devices. Implantable heart pumps are surgically placed within the body to assist or replace the heart's function, providing long-term support for patients with severe heart failure. Extracorporeal heart pumps, on the other hand, are external devices used temporarily, often during surgeries or in critical care settings, to maintain circulation and support cardiac function while the heart recovers or is being repaired.</p></p>
