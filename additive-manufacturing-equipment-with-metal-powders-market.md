<p><p>Additive manufacturing equipment for metal powders encompasses various technologies, primarily including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses high-powered lasers to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with high precision, suitable for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to achieve similar results, often used for larger parts. Other market types may include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, broadening design possibilities.</p></p>
