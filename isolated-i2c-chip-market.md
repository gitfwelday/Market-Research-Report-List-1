<p><p>The isolated I2C chip market primarily features two types: SCK Mode Single Direction and SCK Mode Dual Direction. In Single Direction mode, data transmission occurs unidirectionally, allowing communication from a master to a slave device, ensuring simplicity and reducing noise. Conversely, Dual Direction mode permits bidirectional communication, allowing both master and slave to send and receive data, enhancing flexibility in various applications. Both modes are essential for ensuring robust and secure communication in electrically noisy environments.</p></p>
