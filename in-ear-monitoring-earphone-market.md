<p><ul><li>Dynamic Earphone</li><li>Moving Iron Earphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Ear Monitoring (IEM) earphone market features two main types: dynamic earphones and moving iron earphones. Dynamic earphones utilize a diaphragm and magnet to produce sound, offering robust bass response and a wide soundstage, making them popular among casual listeners. In contrast, moving iron earphones employ a balanced armature driver technology, delivering precise audio reproduction with enhanced clarity and detail, often favored by professionals for monitoring purposes in music and audio production. Each type caters to specific audio preferences and uses.</p></p>
