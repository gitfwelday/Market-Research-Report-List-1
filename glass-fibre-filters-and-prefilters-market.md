<p><p>Glass fibre filters and prefilters are essential components in air filtration systems, categorized primarily into ASHRAE, HEPA, and ULPA types. ASHRAE filters are designed for general air quality improvement, providing medium filtration efficiency. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters capture at least 99.97% of particles, ensuring high-level air purity, making them ideal for healthcare settings. ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters offer even greater efficiency, trapping 99.999% of particles, suitable for critical environments like cleanrooms and laboratories. These filters contribute significantly to maintaining air quality standards.</p></p>
