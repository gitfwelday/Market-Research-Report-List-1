<p><p>The Micro Electro Mechanical System (MEMS) based accelerometer market is categorized into four types: 1-axis, 2-axis, 3-axis, and combo accelerometers. </p><p>1-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along a single direction, making them simple and cost-effective. </p><p>2-axis accelerometers capture acceleration in two dimensions, offering more detail for applications like tilt sensing. </p><p>3-axis accelerometers provide comprehensive motion sensing in all three dimensions, suitable for advanced applications. </p><p>Combo accelerometers integrate features of other sensors, enhancing functionality for complex applications in various industries.</p></p>
