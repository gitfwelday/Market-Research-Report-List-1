<p><p>The PSP (Phosphor Storage Plates) System is a digital radiography technology that utilizes phosphor plates to capture and store images. These plates are exposed to ionizing radiation, which excites the phosphor material, allowing it to store energy. When stimulated by a laser, the captured energy is released as light, processed, and converted into a digital image. This technology is widely used in medical imaging, particularly in dentistry and veterinary fields, due to its advantages over traditional film, including enhanced image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and immediate image retrieval.</p><p>The PSP System Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of digital imaging technologies in healthcare and a rising demand for efficient diagnostic procedures.
