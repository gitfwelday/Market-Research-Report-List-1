<p><strong>The Automotive Hypervisor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare Metal Hypervisors</li><li>Hosted Hypervisors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hypervisor market comprises two primary types: bare metal hypervisors and hosted hypervisors. Bare metal hypervisors operate directly on the hardware, providing high performance and low latency, making them ideal for safety-critical applications in vehicles. In contrast, hosted hypervisors run on top of an operating system, offering more flexibility and easier management, but may have higher latency. Both types enable the virtualization of multiple automotive applications, enhancing functionality and improving resource utilization in modern vehicles.</p></p>
