<p><p>The heat sterilization equipment market includes two main types: dry heat sterilization equipment and moist heat sterilization equipment. Dry heat sterilization utilizes high temperatures to eliminate microorganisms and is commonly used for glassware and metal instruments, ensuring effective sterilization without moisture. In contrast, moist heat sterilization, often achieved through autoclaving, uses steam and pressure to sterilize a wider range of materials, including heat-sensitive items. Each type caters to specific needs in healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and laboratory settings, emphasizing safety and efficacy.</p></p>
