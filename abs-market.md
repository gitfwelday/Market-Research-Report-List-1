<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS market encompasses various polymerization techniques, notably emulsion graft copolymerization and bulk copolymerization. Emulsion graft copolymerization involves polymerizing monomers in an aqueous medium, allowing for better control over properties and reduced environmental impact. Bulk copolymerization, on the other hand, occurs without solvents, facilitating higher molecular weights and improved purity. Both methods are crucial in producing ABS copolymers, which are widely used in applications requiring toughness, impact resistance, and strong aesthetic qualities.</p></p>
