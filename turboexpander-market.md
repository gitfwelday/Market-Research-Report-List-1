<p><ul><li>Radial Turboexpander</li><li>Axial Turboexpander</li><li>Radial-Axial Turboexpander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turboexpander market comprises three main types: radial turboexpanders, axial turboexpanders, and radial-axial turboexpanders. </p><p>Radial turboexpanders are compact and efficient, ideal for applications with limited space and high pressure differentials. Axial turboexpanders excel in high-volume gas processing, offering better efficiency for large-scale operations. Radial-axial turboexpanders combine features of both types, providing versatility for varied applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, contributing to sectors like natural gas processing, refrigeration, and renewable energy, driven by the demand for improved energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
