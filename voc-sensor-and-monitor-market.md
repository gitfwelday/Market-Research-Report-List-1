<p><strong>The VOC Sensor and Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Type</li><li>PID Type</li><li>Metal oxide (MOS) Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC sensor and monitor market comprises various types designed to detect volatile organic compounds. The electrochemical type utilizes chemical reactions to measure specific gases, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. PID (Photoionization Detectors) type sensors measure ionized gases and are effective for a wide range of VOCs. Metal oxide semiconductor (MOS) sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance to detect gases, offering durability and low cost. Other types may include thermal conductivity and infrared-based sensors, each serving different applications in environmental monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
