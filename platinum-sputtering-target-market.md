<p><p>The Platinum Sputtering Target market is divided into two main types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type. Rotary Type targets enable continuous thin film deposition with improved uniformity and efficiency, making them suitable for high-volume production. Conversely, Non-Rotatable Type targets are fixed in place, often used for smaller-scale applications or where rotation isn't feasible. Both types cater to distinct manufacturing requirements in industries like electronics, optics, and surface coating, influencing their selection based on the specific application and desired film characteristics.</p></p>
