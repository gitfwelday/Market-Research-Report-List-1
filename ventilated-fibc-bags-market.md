<p><p>The Ventilated FIBC bags market consists of Two Sides Ventilated and Four Sides Ventilated bags, designed for optimal airflow. Two Sides Ventilated bags feature mesh panels on two opposite sides, facilitating air circulation while providing structural integrity, ideal for bulk materials like grains. In contrast, Four Sides Ventilated bags incorporate mesh panels on all four sides, maximizing ventilation and moisture control, suitable for perishable goods or sensitive products requiring enhanced breathability during storage and transport.</p></p>
