<p><ul><li>Water-Based Polyurethane Concrete Sealers</li><li>Solvent-Based Polyurethane Concrete Sealers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane concrete sealers market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based sealers. Water-based polyurethane sealers offer lower VOC emissions, faster drying times, and easier cleanup, making them ideal for indoor applications and environmentally conscious consumers. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane sealers provide superior durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty outdoor and industrial applications. Both types enhance the longevity of concrete surfaces while offering different advantages based on specific user needs and settings.</p></p>
