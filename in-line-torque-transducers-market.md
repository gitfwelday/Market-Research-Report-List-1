<p><p>In-Line torque transducers are essential devices for measuring torque in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: static torque sensors and dynamic torque sensors. Static torque sensors are designed for measuring torque in stationary systems, providing accurate readings during load testing and calibration. In contrast, dynamic torque sensors are used in rotating systems, capturing real-time torque measurements under operational conditions. Both types play crucial roles in automotive, industrial, and aerospace sectors, ensuring enhanced performance and safety.</p></p>
