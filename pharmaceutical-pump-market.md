<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li><li>Jet Pump</li><li>Air Lift Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical pump market includes various types designed for specific applications. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy for fluid movement, ideal for low-viscosity liquids. Positive displacement pumps move fluid by trapping a fixed amount and forcing it into the discharge, suitable for higher-viscosity fluids. Jet pumps utilize high-velocity fluids to create suction, often used in transferring volatile substances. Air lift pumps use compressed air to move liquids, advantageous in certain biochemical processes. Each type is critical for efficient and precise fluid management in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
