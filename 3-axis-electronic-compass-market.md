<p><ul><li>Hall Effect</li><li>Fluxgate</li><li>Magneto Resistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-axis electronic compass market comprises Hall Effect, Fluxgate, and Magneto Resistive technologies. Hall Effect compasses detect magnetic fields by measuring voltage changes, offering compact and low-power solutions, ideal for consumer electronics. Fluxgate compasses utilize magnetic materials to measure field strengths, providing high accuracy and stability for navigation systems. Magneto Resistive compasses employ changes in resistance in magnetic fields, offering superior sensitivity and robustness, making them suitable for demanding applications like aviation and military use.</p></p>
