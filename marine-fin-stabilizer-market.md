<p><p>The marine fin stabilizer market is divided into two main types: retractable and non-retractable fin stabilizers. Retractable fin stabilizers can be raised or lowered as needed, allowing for efficiency in various sea conditions and reducing drag when not in use. Non-retractable fin stabilizers, on the other hand, remain fixed in position, offering continuous stability and improved comfort on vessels. Both types enhance vessel performance and safety by reducing roll and improving passenger comfort during voyages.</p></p>
