<p><p>The automotive high voltage cables market is categorized into shielded and unshielded cables. Shielded cables are designed with protective layers to minimize electromagnetic interference, ensuring reliable performance in electric and hybrid vehicles. They are crucial for maintaining signal integrity and safety. In contrast, unshielded cables lack these protective layers and are typically lighter and less expensive. They are often used in less critical applications where electromagnetic interference is less of a concern, thus serving a different segment of automotive needs.</p></p>
