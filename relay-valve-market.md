The subtypes include valves with crack pressure less than 2 Psig, which are suitable for low-pressure applications; those ranging from 2 to 4 Psig, ideal for moderate flow conditions; and those exceeding 4 Psig, designed for high-pressure scenarios. Each type serves specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal performance across various applications in plumbing, pneumatic, and hydraulic systems.</p></p>
