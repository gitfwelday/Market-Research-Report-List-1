<p><ul><li>Rule Based</li><li>Conversational AI Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Virtual Assistant market is primarily divided into two types: Rule-Based and Conversational AI-Based assistants. Rule-Based assistants follow predefined scripts and decision trees, providing responses based on specific keywords and intents, making them effective for straightforward tasks. In contrast, Conversational AI-Based assistants leverage advanced machine learning algorithms and natural language processing, enabling more dynamic and human-like interactions. This allows them to understand context, learn from user interactions, and handle complex queries more effectively, enhancing user experience.</p></p>
