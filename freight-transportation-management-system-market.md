<p><p>Freight Transportation Management Systems (TMS) encompass various market types, including rail freight, road freight, ocean freight, and air freight. Rail freight utilizes trains for transporting bulk goods efficiently over long distances, while road freight relies on trucks for flexible, door-to-door delivery. Ocean freight is ideal for large volumes of cargo across international waters, leveraging container ships for cost-effectiveness. Air freight, though more expensive, offers rapid transport for urgent, high-value shipments, ensuring timely delivery across global markets.</p></p>
