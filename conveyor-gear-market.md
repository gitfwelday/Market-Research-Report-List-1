<p><p>The conveyor gear market features various types, including parallel axis gears and angle gears. Parallel axis gears are designed for efficient power transmission in straight-line applications, offering compact designs and minimized torque loss. Angle gears, on the other hand, facilitate power transmission at right angles, enabling flexible system layouts in confined spaces. Both types enhance conveyor system performance, improving speed, efficiency, and reliability, making them essential components in diverse industries such as manufacturing, mining, and logistics.</p></p>
