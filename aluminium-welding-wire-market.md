<p><strong>The Aluminium Welding Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Aluminum Welding Wire</li><li>Al-Mg Alloy Welding Wire</li><li>Al-Si Alloy Welding Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminium welding wire market includes three main types: Pure Aluminum Welding Wire, known for its excellent conductivity and corrosion resistance, is ideal for non-structural applications. Al-Mg Alloy Welding Wire offers enhanced strength and is commonly used in marine and automotive industries due to its resistance to stress corrosion cracking. Al-Si Alloy Welding Wire is favored for its fluidity and low melting point, making it suitable for applications requiring high-quality welds, particularly in the automotive and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
