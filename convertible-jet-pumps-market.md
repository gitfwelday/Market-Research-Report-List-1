<p><ul><li>Shallow Well Type</li><li>Deep Well Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The convertible jet pumps market consists of two primary types: shallow well and deep well pumps. Shallow well jet pumps are designed for water sources located within 25 feet of the surface, using atmospheric pressure to draw water. In contrast, deep well jet pumps are intended for deeper sources, often exceeding 25 feet, requiring a more sophisticated mechanism to create the necessary suction. Both types cater to varying well depths, offering flexibility for residential and agricultural water supply needs.</p></p>
