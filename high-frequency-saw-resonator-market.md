<p><p>The high-frequency Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) resonator market comprises two main types: one-port and two-port resonators. One-port resonators feature a single electrode design, primarily used in applications such as frequency generation and filtering due to their simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, two-port resonators have two electrodes, facilitating more complex signal processing and offering better performance for filters and oscillators. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive systems, catering to varying frequency and performance requirements.</p></p>
