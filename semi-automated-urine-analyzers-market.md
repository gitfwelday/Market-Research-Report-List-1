<p><p>The semi-automated urine analyzers market encompasses various analysis types, including chemical, sediment, microscopy, and flow cytometry analysis. Chemical analysis assesses urine composition through reagent strips for pH, proteins, glucose, and other parameters. Sediment analysis involves examining urine for cells, crystals, and microorganisms, providing insights into renal conditions. Microscopy analysis uses optical methods to visualize sediment components at a cellular level, while flow cytometry analysis quantifies particles in fluid samples, enhancing diagnostic accuracy in urinary health monitoring.</p></p>
