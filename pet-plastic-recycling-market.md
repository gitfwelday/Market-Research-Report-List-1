<p><p>The PET plastic recycling market consists of two main types: mechanical recycling and chemical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves physically processing used PET plastics into granules for reuse in new products, maintaining their polymer structure. In contrast, chemical recycling breaks down PET into its fundamental monomers, allowing for the creation of virgin-like materials. Both methods aim to reduce waste and promote sustainability, but they differ in complexity and the quality of the recycled output, catering to various market needs.</p></p>
