<p><p>The Sequencing Batch Reactor (SBR) activated sludge process market is primarily divided into Continuous Fill Batch Reactor (CFBR) and Intermittent markets. CFBR operates by continuously feeding influent into the reactor while maintaining a batch treatment cycle, allowing for efficient processing and reduced downtime. In contrast, the Intermittent market involves discrete batch cycles where influent is added, treated, and then discharged, making it ideal for systems that can handle variable flows and promote flexibility in treatment operations. Both types optimize wastewater management.</p></p>
